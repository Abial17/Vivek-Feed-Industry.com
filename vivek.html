About Us

About Us: Vivek Feed Industry Vivek Feed Industry is a leading provider of high-quality animal feed products, dedicated to enhancing the nutrition and well-being of livestock. Established with a vision to support the agricultural sector, we pride ourselves on delivering premium feed solutions tailored to meet the diverse nutritional needs of various animals, including poultry, cattle, and aquaculture. Our products are formulated using cutting-edge technology and the latest scientific research, ensuring optimal growth, health, and productivity for livestock. Our commitment to quality is unwavering. At Vivek Feed Industry, we source only the finest raw materials to produce nutrient-rich feed that meets the highest industry standards. We employ rigorous quality control measures at every stage of production, from sourcing ingredients to packaging the final product. This dedication to excellence has earned us the trust of farmers, distributors, and industry professionals across the region. Sustainability and innovation are at the heart of our operations. We continuously invest in research and development to improve our formulations, making them not only more effective but also environmentally friendly. By reducing waste and enhancing the efficiency of feed conversion, we contribute to a more sustainable agricultural ecosystem. Our focus on sustainable practices helps farmers achieve higher yields while minimizing their environmental footprint. Customer satisfaction is central to our mission. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by providing personalized service and support. Our team of experts is always available to offer guidance on feed selection, usage, and livestock management. We understand the challenges faced by farmers, and we are committed to helping them achieve success through reliable, cost-effective solutions. At Vivek Feed Industry, our goal is to be a trusted partner in the growth of the agricultural community. With a strong foundation of quality, innovation, and customer care, we are dedicated to supporting the livestock industry and helping farmers thrive. 1.Nutrient-Rich: Laid by healthy hens that are fed a balanced diet to ensure optimal nutrition and taste.

2.Variety of Sizes: Available in various sizes to meet your cooking and baking needs.

3.Local Delivery: Prompt delivery to ensure maximum freshness for our customers.

Live Broiler Chickens

Feed Production
